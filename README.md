# mastering-business-analyst-case-studies

A curated collection of real-world Business Analyst case studies, frameworks, requirement documents, process flows, stakeholder scenarios, and business problem-solving projects.

## What’s Included

- Business Requirement Documents (BRD)
- Functional Requirement Specifications (FRS)
- User Stories & Acceptance Criteria
- SWOT, PESTLE & GAP Analysis
- BPMN & Process Flow Diagrams
- Agile & Scrum Case Studies
- KPI & Dashboard Use Cases
- SQL & Data Analysis Scenarios
- Stakeholder Management Examples
- End-to-End Business Case Frameworks

## Domains Covered

- Banking & Finance
- Healthcare
- E-commerce
- Retail
- Logistics
- Insurance
- HR Analytics
- SaaS & Technology

## Goal

To build practical Business Analysis skills through structured case studies and industry-oriented problem-solving projects.
